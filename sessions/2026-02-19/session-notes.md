## 重点错题记录

| 涉及知识点 | 原始/错误完整句子 | 纠正后的正确表达 | 深度分析 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Past Participle 拼写** | "She has maken notes" | "She has made notes" | make-made-made (混淆 taken) |
| **Past Participle 拼写** | "would not have forgot it" | "would not have forgotten it" | forget-forgot-forgotten |
| **Parallel Structure** | "to make notes or just listening" | "to make notes or just listen" | 平行结构不一致 (to do vs doing) |
| **Mixed Conditional 时态** | "the cost would be much lower" | "would be" (现在) vs "would have been" (过去) | 虽语法正确，但表达"当时的成本"需用全过去虚拟 |
| **Countable Noun Article** | "waiting for reply" | "waiting for a/the reply" | reply 可数，需加冠词 |
| **Tense (Will vs Is)** | "there won't be a choice" | "there isn't a choice" | 表达"现有的状态(菜单上的选项)"用一般现在时 |
