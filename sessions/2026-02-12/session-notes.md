## 重点错题记录与表现不足详情

| 涉及知识点 | 原始/错误完整句子 | 纠正后的正确表达 | 深度分析 (不足之处) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Collocation (study for)** | "...want to study **for** project management?" | "...want to **learn/study** project management?" | **搭配错误**: `study for` 表"为...备考"，学习某个学科/技能应直接用 `learn/study + 学科`。 |
| **Past Participle** | "Why haven't you **get** this report..." | "Why haven't you **got** this report..." | **动词形式**: `haven't` (助动词) 后必须接过去分词 `got`，不能用原形 `get`。 |
| **Collocation (shared drive)** | "...on **shared disks**" | "...on **the shared drive**" | **职场术语**: 公司网络共享盘是 `the shared drive` (单数特指)，`shared disks` 指物理硬盘。 |
| **Collocation (update files)** | "...the **up-to-date updating** files" | "...the **latest update** files" | **冗余表达**: `up-to-date` 和 `updating` 叠加冗余，用 `latest update` 更简洁。 |
| **Negation + Pronoun** | "We don't have **someone** on the ground..." | "We don't have **anyone** on the ground..." | **否定句代词**: 否定句中不定代词应用 `anyone`，不用 `someone`。 |
| **Collocation (client site)** | "...in the **customer's places**" | "...at the **client site**" | **职场术语**: "客户现场"用 `(at) the client site`，不用 `places` 复数。 |
| **Vocabulary (remotely)** | "...just deal with this **online**?" | "...just deal with it **remotely**?" | **精准度**: "远程处理"用 `remotely` 更精准，`online` 侧重线上。 |
| **Vocabulary (already)** | "...would be solved **earlier**" | "...would **already** be solved" | **语义偏差**: "早就解决了"= 已经解决了 → `already be solved`; `earlier` 表比较。 |

## 涵盖的关键概念

- **否定句中的不定代词**: `someone`(肯定句) → `anyone`(否定句)
- **助动词后接过去分词**: `haven't got`(✅) vs `haven't get`(❌)
- **职场术语积累**: `the shared drive`, `the client site`, `remotely`, `the company intranet`, `production`
- **"早就" 的虚拟语气表达**: `would already be solved`(✅) vs `would be solved earlier`(❌)
