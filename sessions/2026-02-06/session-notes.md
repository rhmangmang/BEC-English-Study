- **理解程度**: 极高。特别是在选择疑问句 `Are you on your mobile or your office phone?` 中表现非常自然。

## 重点错题记录 (Error Log for Targeted Practice)

| 涉及知识点 | 原始错误句子 | 纠正后的正确表达 | 错误类型 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mixed Conditional** | "...you **would have not been** so successful" | "...you **would not be** so successful" | 时态选择错误 (误用为全过去假设) |
| **Question Word Order** | "Why **did not you** try..." | "Why **didn't you** try..." | 助动词缩写位置错误 |
| **Tag Question Match** | "Trying ... would help, **doesn't it**?" | "Trying ... would help, **wouldn't it**?" | 反义疑问句动词不匹配 |
| **Pronoun Clarity** | "Because of **its** bad connection..." | "Because of **the** bad connection..." | 指代不明 (its 无明确先行词) |
| **Tense (Duration)** | "Why **are you** on the phone **the whole day**?" | "Why **have you been** on your mobile **all day**?" | 时态欠妥 (持续状态应用完成时) |
| **Articles** | "...before checking with **finance department**" | "...before checking with **the Finance Department**" | 冠词缺失 (特定部门需加 the) |
| **Double -ing** | "**Trying changing** our sales strategy..." | "**Trying to change**..." | 语感欠佳 (避免两个 -ing 连用) |
| **Prepositions** | "...at **his office at lunch break**" | "...at **his office during his lunch break**" | 时间介词误用 (强调期间用 during) |

## 识别出的知识差距

| 主题 | 严重程度 | 备注 |
| :--- | :------- | :--- |
| **Double -ing Avoidance** | 低 | 需建立“动名词后接不定式”或“动名词后接名词”的避雷意识，如 `Trying to change`。 |
| **Article Precision (Dept)** | 中 | 职场指代特定部门（Finance, HR）时常遗漏定冠词 `the`。 |
| **Present Perfect for 'All day'** | 中 | 描述从早到现在的持续状态时，需强化使用 `have been` 而非 `are`。 |
